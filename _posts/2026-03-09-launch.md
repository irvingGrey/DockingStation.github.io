# The Decima MAS: The 'Private War' Against Gibraltar

A historical overview of the unconventional naval operations conducted by the Italian **Xa Flottiglia MAS** (10th Light Flotilla) during the Second World War, focusing on their strategic campaign against the British stronghold of Gibraltar.

## Project Overview
This repository documents the evolution of underwater warfare and the specific raids carried out by the Decima MAS. The unit achieved distinction through the development of novel maritime technologies and "human torpedoes" (*Maiale*), pioneering the use of frogmen in offensive operations.

### Key Operational Theaters
The unit's reach extended across the Mediterranean and beyond:
* **Primary Target:** Gibraltar
* **Secondary Locations:** North Africa, Malta, Alexandria, Turkey, and the Black Sea.

---

## Technical Innovation
The Decima MAS was characterized by its departure from traditional naval doctrine, utilizing:
1.  **Human Torpedoes (SLC):** Submersible craft designed to deliver frogmen directly to enemy hulls.
2.  **Specialized Frogmen:** Elite divers trained for clandestine sabotage.
3.  **Covert Submersibles:** Transport vessels used to infiltrate protected harbors.
